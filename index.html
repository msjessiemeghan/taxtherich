Assumptions & sources

This is an educational simulation, not a budget forecast. Real-world revenue depends on behavioral responses, avoidance, and legal design; program costs vary widely across studies. Each number below uses a defensible midpoint from published research so you can weigh the trade-offs. Medicare for All is shown as its gross new federal cost — most analyses find it largely replaces existing private spending and can produce net national savings.
